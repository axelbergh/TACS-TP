How to integrate Objectify into a Maven project
Updated Nov 4, 2010 by latch...@gmail.com

Objectify artifacts compatible with Maven 2 are available from the Objectify Maven repository.

Repository

To add Objectify to your Maven project, add this to your pom.xml: 

  <dependencies>
    <dependency>
      <groupId>com.googlecode.objectify</groupId>
      <artifactId>objectify</artifactId>
      <version>2.2.2</version>
    </dependency>
  </dependencies>

  <repositories>
    <repository>
      <id>objectify-appengine</id>
      <url>http://objectify-appengine.googlecode.com/svn/maven</url>
    </repository>
  </repositories>
